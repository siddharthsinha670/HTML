Our Story

Born from a
Passion for
the Perfect Cup

BRÛLÉ was born in a small garage in 2018, when founder Mara Delacour returned from three years traveling the coffee regions of Ethiopia, Colombia, and Indonesia. She came back with one obsession: bringing those extraordinary flavors to her city.

We source directly from farmers we know by name, roast in small batches every morning, and pull every shot with intention. This isn't just coffee — it's a ritual.

How We Do It

The Craft Behind
Every Cup

01
🌍
Origin Selection
We travel to farms across 14 countries, cupping thousands of lots to select only the finest single-origin harvests each season.
02
🔥
Artisan Roasting
Roasted in 10kg batches on our vintage Probat drum roaster. Every profile tuned by hand to unlock the bean's unique character.
03
⚖️
Precision Dialing
Our baristas dial each espresso shot using refractometers and extraction scales. Science meets intuition at every step.
04
🎨
Your Perfect Cup
From the first pour to the final latte art flourish, every cup is made with the same care as the first one we ever brewed.
